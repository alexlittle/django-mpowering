Uploading resources to the COVID-19 Library is a 2 step process:

  1. Add the information about the resource. Title, description, tags etc
  2. Upload the actual resource files and/or links

Once the resource has been uploaded it will go through our review process.

Specific information about the fields on each step is shown below, the required fields are shown with an asterisk.

Step 1

On this page you can enter the meta data/tags about your resource:

  • Title * - the title of your resource
  • Organisations * - the organisation/s which created this resource (at least one must be entered)
  • Description * - a short description of the resource, max 150 words
  • Image - an image to display alongside your resource
  • Health Domain * - the health domain/s your resource is most relevant for (at least one must be selected)
  • Resource Type * - the type of your resource (at least one must be selected)
  • Study Time - the approximate study time for learners
  • Audience * - the target audience/s (at least one must be selected)
  • Geography * - enter the geographical area/s your resource could be used in (at least one must be entered)
  • Languages * - enter the language/s your resource uses (at least one must be entered)
  • Device * - the devices that your resource can be used on (at least one must be selected)
  • License * - the license your resource is available under (select one)
  • Attribution - specific text you would like to be used for the attribution for this resource
  • Other tags - any other relevant tags that could be useful for users when searching for your resource

Step 2

On this page you can upload the actual resource files and/or links. For each file or link, complete the form and click the 'Add' button. Once you have added all the files/links, click on 'Finished'

  • Title - a title for the resource file/link
  • File - select the file to upload
  • Url - enter the link/url
