Anyone is welcome to share content resources on COVID-19 Library!

If you believe your content meets our criteria, we would like to hear from you. Just go to our ‘Add a Resource’ link and you’ll find out how your content could potentially reach hundreds of thousands of health workers across the world.

Are you interested in becoming an COVID-19 Library Content Partner?

This is a more formal relationship with COVID-19 Library and COVID-19 Digital Classroom.

As a Content Partner, we will create an COVID-19 Library partner page with your logo, a short description of your organisation and a link back to your home page. We will also help connect with you other partners who may want to use your content or commission new content from you. Content Partners should have an internal quality assurance and review process in place, as this will help us fast-track your COVID-19 Library resources through our content review process.

The criteria for becoming a content partner are:

  • Commitment to using open content licensing (Creative Commons) and sharing your digital health training materials on COVID-19 Library. Watch our video on Creative Commons for Global Health
  • Robust, well-documented internal quality assurance and review processes - this helps us to fast-track your COVID-19 Library resources through our content review process
  • Agreement to being an ambassador for COVID-19 Library - promoting its use by implementers, health workers and others

To apply to become an COVID-19 Library Content Partner, please contact us. All Content Partners should send a letter of commitment, not to exceed one page, which documents how they meet these criteria and their motivation for becoming a Content Partner.
